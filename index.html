About

This application is designed for the international students who will be attending Fanshawe. The application has different online and offline features which will help the student to find his/her way around the school and other important facilities.

Features

This phone application includes a variety of information which may be useful not only to international, but also for local students starting their first year.

  • Checklist: This is a list of all the steps a student has to complete to assure they are enrolled in college. The list also includes links to other important student related webstites.
  • Contacts: A contact directory is included. Contacts which are listed to be reached over the phone or email include the Biz Booth, the library, etc.
  • Advisors: Advisors are assigned to all of the international students depending of where they come from. It is important for the students to know their names and contact information.
  • Locations: This is a list of different stores with their respective bus routes from the college.
  • Map: A map of the college with their room description is included.
  • Housing: Students will most likely need to know where to live when they arrive at Canada.
  • Entertainment: This is a list of bars and other places in London Downtown where students can go when they have free time.

This app was developed over roughly 4 months.
